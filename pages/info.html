About The Roost

What is “The Roost”?

The Roost is a game room opened to all enrolled students at The University of Texas at San Antonio. We are located on the second floor of the HEB University Center, on the main campus. The Roost offers a variety of games such as pool, air hockey, foosball, ping pong and board games. The Roost also has five different gaming systems, Wii, XBOX 360, PlayStation 3, Playstation 4, and Xbox One with over 50 different games to choose from. So what are you waiting for? Come and stop on by! All you need is a valid UTSA student I.D.
